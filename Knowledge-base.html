macOS 10.11 tells me that QOwnNotes.app is damaged and wants to move it to the trash bin

After copying the app to the Applications folder open a terminal and run:
sudo xattr -r -d com.apple.quarantine /Applications/QOwnNotes.app
You will be prompted for your password. Enter it and the issue should be fixed.
